Beginner Courses

Introduction to Organic Gardening

Monthly on Saturdays 10am - 3pm £45

Learn the fundamentals of organic gardening, from soil preparation to planting your first crops. Perfect for complete beginners.

Container Gardening

First Sunday of month 11am - 2pm £35

Discover how to grow organic produce in small spaces using containers, perfect for urban gardeners.

Organic Vegetable Growing

Take your vegetable growing to the next level with advanced organic techniques for higher yields.

Beekeeping Basics

Permaculture Design
