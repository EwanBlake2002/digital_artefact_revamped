Organic ME

Organic ME is a growing company located in Kelso, in the Borders region of Scotland. The business specializes in selling organic food products, household items, homeware, and clothing. A key feature of their offering is their own organic honey, which is produced locally and sold in their café. Additionally, Organic ME hosts regular monthly organic gardening courses for small groups, fostering community engagement and promoting sustainable practices.

The company sources its products from local organic farmers, ensuring high quality and sustainability. With a commitment to providing handmade, eco-friendly goods, Organic ME stands out as a unique establishment in the region. While they face competition from larger organic product retailers across the UK, their localized approach and dedication to organic living offer a distinctive advantage.

Organic ME currently uses social media channels like Facebook, Instagram, and YouTube to connect with customers. However, they are now planning to launch a dedicated website to expand their reach, attract new customers, and support tourism in the area. The site will feature product listings, café and shop price lists, special offers, a photo gallery, and video content, along with opportunities for customers to subscribe to newsletters.

At its core, Organic ME values sustainability, community engagement, and quality. By exclusively offering organic and handmade products, the company minimizes its environmental footprint while supporting local producers. This ethos extends to its café, which serves organic honey produced in-house, further emphasizing a farm-to-table experience.
